wireframes_v2

addition of features:

	- sign up with google account

	- remove a product from the offering list
	- transaction acknowledgement (both for the collecting person and the suplier)
	- cancel a transaction (when the products has the accepted statuses)

review of the products statuses in the shopping cart
Offered products are either :
	- available (they're published online but no one has collected it, possibility to remove it)
	- accepted (someone has requested to collect it -> send message functionality & removal option & change the status to delivered once the transaction is done)
	- delivered (transaction aknowledgement : the transaction has been done)
Collected products are either :
	- pending (waiting to be collected, possibility to cancel the transaction)
	- collected (transaction acknowledgement)
